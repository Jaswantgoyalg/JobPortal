Greenland Investment Mangement

Mumbai, Maharashtra, India

about Greenland Investment Mangement Company

Greenland Investment Management is a Mumbai-based systematic global arbitrage hedge fund manager managing over USD 1 billion in assets under management. We started our journey in 2013 as a proprietary trading firm and in 2016 opened our first arbitrage fund - the Greenland Global Fund - for external capital. In 2019, we launched our dedicated commodity arbitrage fund, Greenland Global Fund II, as a carve out from GGF

Today, our investor base primarily consists of pensions, endowments, independent asset managers and other institutional investors. We continue to invest significant proprietary capital in our funds alongside our investors to maintain a strong alignment of interests.

We specialize in high-frequency currency arbitrage and mid-frequency commodity arbitrage strategies traded across global exchanges. Proprietary datasets, risk-managed systematic portfolio construction and in-house ultra-low latency trading systems help us identify and capture these arbitrage opportunities effectively and consistently.

We are always on the lookout for exceptional talent to join our close-knit team. Please reach out if you would like to explore possibilities.
