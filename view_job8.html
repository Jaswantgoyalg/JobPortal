    About the job

  • We are seeking a skilled Ping Identity Engineer with experience in AWS and React to join our dynamic team. As a Ping Identity Engineer, you will play a crucial role in designing, implementing, and maintaining our identity and access management solutions.

    Responsibilities:

  • Design, configure, and deploy Ping Identity solutions, including PingFederate, Ping Access, and Ping Directory.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to integrate Ping solutions into our existing infrastructure.
  • Implement Single Sign-On (SSO) and Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) solutions.
  • Troubleshoot and resolve issues related to identity and access management.
  • Develop and maintain custom authentication and authorization flows.
  • Work closely with AWS services, including IAM, Cognito, and Lambda.

    • Stay up to date with industry trends and best practices related to identity and access management
