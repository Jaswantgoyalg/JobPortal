ETL Vertica Developer

Tech Mahindra

Chennai, Tamilnadu, India

salary

60k Per Month

job type

Full Time

schedule

Remote

    Qualification:

  • Proven experience with Vertica database management and optimization.
  • Demonstrated ability in setting up and managing ETL pipelines.
  • Proficient in programming with Node.js and Python.
  • Strong understanding of VPC, authentication protocols, RBAC, and data synchronization mechanisms.
  • Excellent problem-solving skills and the ability to work collaboratively.

    Detailed Job Description

  • Develop and optimize ETL processes to ensure efficient data extraction, transformation, and loading.
  • Manage and enhance Vertica database systems to ensure high performance and reliability.
  • Utilize Node.js and Python for scripting and automating tasks within the data ecosystem.
  • Implement and maintain cloud-based solutions, with a focus on Google Cloud services.
  • Design and manage Virtual Private Cloud (VPC) setups, authentication systems, role-based access controls (RBAC), and data synchronization tasks.
  • Work closely with infrastructure engineers to integrate database and cloud solutions into the broader technology landscape.
  • Contribute to continuous improvement initiatives by staying current with emerging technologies and industry best practices.
